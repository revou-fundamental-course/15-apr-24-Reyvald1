Temperature in °C/°F

How to Use "Kalkulator Suhu"

1. Input a number at “Input Number”

2. Click "To °C" to convert to Celsius

3. Click "To °F" to convert to Fahrenheit

4. Click "Reset" to clear the conversion result

How to Convert Celcius to Fahrenheit

S(°F) = (S(°C) × (9/5) + 32)

or

S(°F) = (S(°C) × 1.8) + 32

How to Convert Fahrenheit to Celcius

S(°C) = ((S(°F) - 32) × 5/9)

or

S(°C) = ((S(°F) - 32) × 0.55556)
